SignCam

SignCam helps deaf people to communicate more easily with non-deaf people when they speak in American Sign Language. And also SignCam helps people learn American Sign Language. American Sign Language (ASL) is a complete, natural language that has the same linguistic properties as spoken languages, with grammar that differs from English.

Join us in continuing our efforts to bridge the gap between the Deaf and hearing worlds. As we expand, your donation enables us to create more innovative theatrical experiences, develop a production hub for TV and film, and continue with our educational programs that serve the cultural, educational, linguistic communication and social needs of Deaf children.
